Nikon Photographs

With my Nikon D5200, I mainly like to use two built in camera settings for my photos: silhouette and sunset. These two settings give me exactly the look I want by amplifying the contrast between the shadows and colors in the image. They also bring new interesting perspectives by dramatizing the images in a way that keeps the integrity of the scene but also deviates from reality.
