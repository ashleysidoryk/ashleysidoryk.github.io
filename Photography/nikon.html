Shot With Nikon

The Nikon D5200 was the first DSLR I received as a gift in high school after I became more interested in photography. There were two built in camera settings I used without fail: silhouette and sunset. They both amplify the shadows and colors within the frame, bringing about new perspectives by dramatizing the images in a way that keeps the integrity of the scene but also deviates from reality. Through these settings, I discovered the style of photography I have come to love shooting.
