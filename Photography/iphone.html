iPhone Photographs

A technique I like to use when shooting images with my iPhone is to play around with the lighting by adjusting the brightness. When you tap on the screen to focus, a sun pops up. With that sun, you can drag it up to increase the brightness of the image and down to decrease. I usually tend to drag the sun icon down a little to create a darker ambiance in my images.
