Wander


Wander was created to tackle the issue of feeling unsafe on college campuses at night. While walking is the prime form of transportation on campuses because vehicle transportation and ride sharing services are not as efficient or affordable, many students feel unsafe walking alone at night. Wander is an app that connects college students in the same area with similar commutes. It is designed to provide safe walking transportation on campus and allow students to feel more comfortable while deterring crimes that target isolated individuals.

Fridgloo

Fridgloo was designed to allow users to have access to their fridge at any given moment. The all-inclusive app oragnizes everything food in that it gives users the ability to create grocery lists, add items from their shopping carts directly into their fridges, and look up recipes based on what items they have readily available. Not only does the app allow users to add and remove food items from their fridges, it also allows for expiration date and food catergory inputs in order to alert users when certain food items are about to expire. Fridgloo was created to help users keep their fridges more organized in order to reduce food waste and make grocery shopping less of a headache.
